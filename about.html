About Me

Hi there! My name is Eric Bravo and I'm a middle school science teacher with a passion for web development. I earned my B.S. in Nutritional Science from Rutgers University and later completed the Alternate Route program to obtain my teaching certification. Currently, I'm taking a web development course through Udemy to enhance my skills in this field.

Before becoming a teacher, I worked as a technician and sales representative for Verizon Wireless. After earning my degree, I offered nutrition counseling and personal training services at the Robert Wood Johnson Fitness & Wellness Center. Eventually, I decided to pursue my passion for education and became a middle school science teacher in the Edison school district where I currently work.

In my free time, I enjoy staying active through fitness, reading, comics, sports, gaming, and cooking. I'm also an avid coder and enjoy working on web development projects whenever I can. My ultimate goal is to find a part-time job as a web developer while continuing my teaching career.

As an educator and web developer, I'm passionate about using technology to enhance the learning experience for my students and to build innovative and user-friendly websites. I'm always looking for ways to grow my skills and contribute to the tech community.

Thanks for stopping by and getting to know me a bit better. If you have any questions or would like to connect, feel free to reach out to me.
